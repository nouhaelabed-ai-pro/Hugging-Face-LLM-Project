Harnessing Hugging Face Models with LangChain

Hugging Face offers thousands of freely accessible language models, providing powerful tools for a wide range of natural language processing tasks. In this project, we seamlessly integrate Hugging Face models into LangChain, enabling us to load and utilize these models efficiently.

By leveraging LangChain's capabilities, we demonstrate how to make predictions using a Hugging Face model, illustrating the ease and effectiveness of this integration.
